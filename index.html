My name is Justin Hurson, I'm a 19 years old student in computer science at the University of Bordeaux, and this is my art-related portfolio.

I like to think of myself as a very creative person, in my free time, I do a lot of pixelart, sometimes related to videogames projects. I also like to do generative art as I'm still a programmer above all.
Less frequently, I happen to make 3D modelisation with Blender, 2D illustration and photo/video montage on various apps. I also love Frontend web programming, but some people say it can't be art...

This website will let you have a look at what I love to do and what I'm proud of, I really hope you'll like it !
